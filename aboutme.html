About Zuriel Atr

Zuriel Atr

Zuriel Atr is an artist and illustrator, I started drawing at the age for 16 when I realized how exciting drawing can be and it was the only thing I was doing and I wanted to learn because i didn't know how to draw, so I started my self-taught carrer researching a lot, practicing a lot, finding many ways to reach what I want to be, a conceptual artist or a professional illustrator.

I am currently studying graphic design and drawing in order to make a living from my passion, which is drawing, and for this I am starting to show more of my work, I make fanarts, I have also created my own characters.

My favorite character is Tear's Avatar, which has great story behind, you can see it on my Instagram!
